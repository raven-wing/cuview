CU View puts your ClickUp tasks directly on your home screen as an Android widget.

Pick any ClickUp view (space → folder → list → view) and its tasks appear on your home screen, auto-refreshing every 15 minutes. Tap a task to open it in the ClickUp app or browser.

Features:
• Connect via ClickUp OAuth — no API token copy-pasting
• Browse your workspace hierarchy to select any list view
• Auto-refresh every 15 minutes via WorkManager
• Manual refresh with the refresh button
• Shows up to 15 tasks; stale cache preserved on network failure
• Dark and light mode support

No account required beyond an existing ClickUp workspace.
